Snake

A version of the classic snake game written in javascript. Eating apples will increase the length of your snake. Running into walls or the snake's trail will result in a death. Try to eat as many apples as you can!

Guess the Word

Test your knowledge of the English language before forming the frowning face of failure. Guess letters to fill in the blanks and form the mystery word. Play as many times as you want, words are generated randomly!
